BIO

via GIPHY

Giacomo Bignardi born in the humanities, with a degree in Drama, Art and Music Studies from the University of Bologna. He was then raised by scientists while doing a minor in Brain and Mind, leaving home to research at the Complex Trait Genetic department at the Vrije Universiteit in Amsterdam. After, he tried to grow up in London, studying Neuroaesthetics at Goldsmiths, University of London, and collaborating with the NTR, at the Biological Psychology Dept. (which is still in Amsterdam, he loves that city). All grown up, he is interested in aesthetics experiences, what they mean, and their underlying sources of etiological variation. Especially in the genetic ones.

This website is about the wanderings on the science and the art that surround the genetic architecture of aesthetic experiences.
